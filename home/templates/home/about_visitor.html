What To Fix: Princeton is here to help change what you care about

Our mission

WTF:Princeton aims to serve as a platform through which Princeton students can suggest ideas for change to USG and support others' ideas. By connecting the students amongst themselves and with USG, WTF:Princeton hopes to begin a conversation for change with the whole student body which will hopefully lead to a better campus life for all.
