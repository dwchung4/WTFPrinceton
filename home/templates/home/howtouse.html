How to use WTF: Princeton

How it works

Every petition is in one of four states: Active, Expired, Pending, or Completed.

An active petition is one that is still able to be voted on by students.

An expired petition is one that did not meet the required number of votes in the given time and is now unvotable.

A pending petition is one that met the required number of votes and is to be considered by USG.

A completed petition is one that USG has finished considering.

So for change to happen, a petition must go from Active to Pending to Completed.

How to create a new petition

Step 1

Sign in using CAS!

Step 2

Create new petition
Click the 'Create new petition' button!

Step 3

Our petition form will then ask for a title, categorization, and description of your idea. Feel free to elaborate as much as you need to, but we can only keep track of 500 characters!

How to vote and comment on others' petitions

Step 1

Sign in using CAS!

Step 2


Click the big blue thumb to vote on petitions you support! Note: you cannot vote for your own, nor vote for any other petition more than once!

Step 3

From the homepage, write your comments and click 'Comment'!

You are now ready to use WTF: Princeton! Go change the campus!
